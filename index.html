Projects

Air Traffic Management Visual Analysis System
Cooperate with Boeing China and North China Air Traffic Control. Our visual analysis system can display and analyze the flight status of the aircraft based on the flight path, aircraft trajectory data and A-CDM ( Airport Collaborative Decision Making) data, and on the ground each stage of the situation, through the visual form of animation to help us analyze the flight rules and the delay of the aircraft.
Fake Base Station Visual Analysis System
Cooperate with the 360 Skyeye team to develop a visual analysis system based on the mobile phone malicious SMS records collected by 360 mobile security guards. Using the log of the mobile phone connecting to the base station and the location of the real base station to analyze the movement trajectory of the fake base station and help the police to arrest criminals.
Real-Time & History Bitcoin Translation Visualization
Visualizing bitcoin translation by using node-link graph. Getting data from WebSocket API. Each node stands for a transaction entity(one or more addresses).
Real-Time History

Visualizing crime data in San Francisco. Analyzing and discovering crime patterns by temporal-spatial filtering.
Visualization of Air Quality in Beijing
Visualizing air quality in Beijing. Analyzing and discovering pollution status by temporal-spatial filtering.
